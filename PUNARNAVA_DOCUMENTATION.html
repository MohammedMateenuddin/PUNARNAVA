🌍 What is PUNARNAVA?

PUNARNAVA is a mobile-friendly website that helps people recycle their old phones, laptops, and other electronic devices in the smartest way possible. Think of it like Swiggy — but instead of ordering food, you're selling your old electronics to certified recyclers near you.

You take a photo of your old device, and our AI (artificial intelligence — a computer brain that can "see" images) instantly tells you what valuable metals are inside it, how much it's worth as scrap, and connects you to the nearest recycler who will pay you for it.

🤖 The AI Brain — How It Works (No Code)

When you take a photo of your old phone, here's what happens:

1
Photo is prepared — Your image is converted into a format the AI can read (like translating a picture into a language the computer understands). This happens instantly on your phone.
2
Sent to Google's Gemini AI — The same AI that powers Google Lens. We tell it: "Identify this device. List every material with weights. Calculate scrap value in ₹. Flag toxic materials. Give disassembly steps."
3
AI analyzes and responds — Gemini examines the shape, ports, buttons, logos, and sends back a detailed report with device name, materials, weights, and safety info.
4
Live prices applied — Today's actual metal prices are fetched from a commodity service and multiplied by each material's weight for accurate ₹ values.
5
Results displayed — Beautiful results card appears in 3-5 seconds with everything you need to know.
Backup systems: If AI fails → backup AI model tries. If both fail → built-in database of 6 device types provides estimated data. The app NEVER crashes.

💰 Business Model — 5 Revenue Streams

1. Recycler Subscriptions (Main Income)

Recyclers pay monthly fees for platform access — like restaurants pay Swiggy. Three tiers: ₹499, ₹1,999, ₹9,999/month. Just 100 recyclers on Professional = ₹2 lakh/month recurring revenue.

2. Transaction Commission (5-8%)

Every time a recycler claims a device, we take a small cut — like how Swiggy takes a % from every order. On a ₹1,800 laptop: we earn ₹90-144.

3. Corporate E-Waste Contracts

Companies like Infosys and TCS have thousands of old devices to dispose of legally. We manage the entire process for an annual fee.

4. Carbon Credits

Every recycled device prevents measurable CO2. We track all CO2 saved across users and sell carbon credits in bulk at ₹1,000-2,000 per tonne.

5. AI Scanner as a Service

Our AI scanner can be licensed to insurance companies, logistics firms, and recycling apps in other countries — like how Google Maps is used inside Ola and Zomato.

❓ Judge & Investor FAQ

Q: How is this different from existing e-waste apps?

Most apps are just directories. We use real-time AI vision to identify materials INSIDE a device, calculate scrap value using LIVE prices, and gamify the experience with points and leaderboards.

Q: How do you make money?

Five streams: recycler subscriptions (₹499-9,999/mo), transaction commissions (5-8%), corporate contracts, carbon credits, and AI scanner licensing.

Q: What stops someone from copying this?

Three moats: AI improves with every scan (data network effect), recycler relationships take time to build, and the user-recycler flywheel is hard to replicate.

Q: Is the AI accurate?

85-98% confidence for common devices. Every scan shows the confidence score. If AI fails, a fallback database ensures the app never crashes.

Q: Can this work outside Bengaluru?

Yes — entirely cloud-based. Works anywhere with internet. We plan Hindi, Tamil, Telugu support. Recycler onboarding scales city by city.

Q: How did you build this?

AI-assisted development with React, Firebase, Google Gemini AI, and a metals pricing API. Built in under 2 weeks, proving small teams can build enterprise-grade products.
